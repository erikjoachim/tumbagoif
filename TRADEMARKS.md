software, but it does **not** grant permission to use the club's
identity or branding.

- Club name

- Logos
- Badges
- Icons used as official branding
- Word marks
- Slogans
- Team names
- Jerseys and merchandise designs
- Graphic profile
- Visual identity
- Marketing material
- Official photographs

## You May

- Fork this repository.
- Modify the software.
- Deploy your own instance.
- Use the software commercially.
- Build your own projects based on the source code.
- Contribute improvements back to this repository.

## You May Not

Without prior written permission, you may not:

- Use the club's names to imply endorsement or affiliation.
- Use the official logos or badges.
- Redistribute official branding assets.
- Present your fork as an official club project.
- Use the visual identity in a way that suggests your project is maintained or approved by the club.

## Naming Forks

If you fork or redistribute this software, you should choose your own
project name and provide your own branding unless you have explicit
permission to use the club's identity.

## Questions

Questions regarding branding or permission to use club assets may be
directed to the club board at [info@tumbagoif.se](mailto:info@tumbagoif.se).
